Typography

Consistent heading and body scale.

Display type is used for hero and section headings; body text stays on the same sans family.

Display

Fraunces, 3rem to 5.6rem for hero copy and larger section titles.

Body

Space Grotesk, 0.78rem to 1.1rem for labels, body text, and supporting copy.
